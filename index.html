Welcome home

Cova is more than just a co-living space. We are a community of people just like you. People who are inspired by connecting and living a similar way of life. A life enjoyed - happier, brighter, simpler.

Discover a place where you belong.

Being part of a community matters. Not just any community, but a diverse and inclusive community that encourages sharing and connecting.

Our weekly social events will provide you with instant access to a network of diverse and ambitious professionals just like you.
